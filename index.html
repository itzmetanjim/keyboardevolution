What is this?

This is a tool to find the keyboard layout that gives the least amount of strain on your hands and fingers.

It uses genetic algorithms to optimize the keyboard layout and Finite Element Analysis on the hands to evaluate the total strain on the hands after typing the entire training set and minimizes that.
